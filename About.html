SOMI FOUNDATION

Youth have power

OUR STORY

Somi Foundation was named after Laurier Somi's late grandmother, Rodah Somi Mutulis, whom she is named after. The foundation is based on the belief that youth have the power to impact communities. Thus, they need to be aware of opportunities to aid their growth. Our goal is to create a community of 2000+ empowered youth by the year 2031. We educate the youth on available opportunities both nationally and internationally and in exchange, the youth we empower, help us to aid Kenyan communities from creating employment opportunities to holding donations to communities affected by natural calamities and even educating children in orphanages on how they too can play a part in environmental conservation So far, we have partnered with over 50 youth led organisations to donate to areas affected by natural calamities, visited over 30 high schools to educate them on available opportunities for growth and planted over 40 trees in various children's homes across the country. Somi Foundation is the home away from home for youth who dream of impacting their communities.
