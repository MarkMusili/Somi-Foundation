Join Our Cause Today

Discover how you can make a difference in our community—whether by partnering with us, becoming a member, volunteering, or donating. Together, we can bring hope to children, support families in crisis, and empower the youth. Let’s build a stronger, more inclusive community.

PARTNER

Partner with Somi Foundation

By partnering with Somi Foundation, you join a dedicated team addressing critical needs in vulnerable communities. Together, we can extend our reach to more children’s homes, families affected by natural disasters, and educational institutions. Partnerships allow us to share resources, knowledge, and connections to uplift communities and foster resilience. Whether you’re a corporation, NGO, or local organization, your partnership helps us deliver impactful programs where they are most needed. .

MEMBER

Become a Member

Joining Somi Foundation as a member means you’re part of a passionate community dedicated to creating change. Members gain access to a unique membership package, which includes exclusive Somi Foundation merchandise and discounts on event tickets. You’ll also connect with like-minded individuals who share your commitment to giving back, and benefit from personalized career opportunities, including scholarships, networking, and mentorship. Becoming a member isn’t just about supporting a cause—it’s about being part of a community that empowers each other to make an impact.

VOLUNTEER

Join Our Volunteer Team

Volunteering at Somi Foundation means being at the heart of our work in communities. From visiting children’s homes to organizing school engagement activities, our volunteers are hands-on in making an impact. As a volunteer, you’ll bring relief, joy, and inspiration to people’s lives, whether through disaster relief, community outreach, or educational support. If you want a fulfilling way to contribute your time and skills, volunteering with Somi Foundation offers a powerful way to connect with and uplift others.

DONATE

Support Our Mission

Donations fuel Somi Foundation’s ability to reach those most in need. Contributions allow us to provide essential resources during our visits to children’s homes, deliver immediate relief to families affected by disasters, and fund school engagement programs. Every donation, no matter the amount, helps us expand our programs and bring support to more people. By donating, you help us sustain our mission and respond effectively to community needs, fostering a brighter future for all.
