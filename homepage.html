SOMI FOUNDATION

Somi Foundation is a youth-driven nonprofit organization founded by Laurier Somi Amunga. We are committed to empowering young people with the tools and opportunities they need to drive meaningful change in their communities. By partnering with youth-led organizations, we strive to create a network of youth who can uplift Kenyan communities through education, environmental conservation, and humanitarian efforts.

OUR PURPOSE

At Somi Foundation, our mission is to empower the next generation of Kenyan youth to become agents of change by providing them with knowledge, resources, and opportunities for personal and community growth. Our vision is to create a community of over 2,000+ empowered young leaders by 2031, who will lead sustainable initiatives across Kenya, fostering environmental conservation, education, and social well-being..
