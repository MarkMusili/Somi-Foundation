SOMI FOUNDATION

Somi Foundation is a youth-driven nonprofit organization founded by Laurier Somi Amunga. We are committed to empowering young people with the tools and opportunities they need to drive meaningful change in their communities. By partnering with youth-led organizations, we strive to create a network of youth who can uplift Kenyan communities through education, environmental conservation, and humanitarian efforts.

OUR PURPOSE

At Somi Foundation, our mission is to empower the next generation of Kenyan youth to become agents of change by providing them with knowledge, resources, and opportunities for personal and community growth. Our vision is to create a community of over 2,000+ empowered young leaders by 2031, who will lead sustainable initiatives across Kenya, fostering environmental conservation, education, and social well-being..

WHAT WE DO

We work hand-in-hand with youth-led organizations to implement impactful projects. Our initiatives include: Environmental Conservation: Planting trees and promoting sustainability in communities and schools. Youth Empowerment: Visiting schools to inform students about educational and employment opportunities. Community Support: Donating essential items to areas affected by natural disasters and providing resources to children’s homes.

FOUNDER

The world’s growth depends on today’s and tomorrow’s youth. Surrounded by opportunities and talented young people, Somi Foundation connects these opportunities to the youth. In return, they become changemakers impacting Kenya, Africa, and the world. Together, we create employment, aid disaster-affected areas, educate on climate change, and shape global thinking. If you’re a young changemaker, this space is for you to thrive. I can’t wait to see the impact we, as Somi, will have on our communities. We are the future, we are Somi.
