Somi Foundation
Brief History
It was founded in April 2020 to provide masks to street children who had no means of preventing themselves from the risk of Covid 19.
Laurier Somi Amunga
Brief History
"Somi Foundation was named after my late grandmother, Rodah Somi Mutulis, whom I am named after. The foundation is based on the belief that youth have power to impact communities. Our goal is to create a community of 2000 empowered youth by the year 2031. We have so far partnered with over 50 youth led organisations to donate to areas affected by natural calamities, visited over 30 high schools to educate them on available opportunities for growth and planted over 40 trees in various children's homes across the country. Somi Foundation is the home away from home for communities in Kenya, and all this is because of the youth."
